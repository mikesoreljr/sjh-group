Market Focus

Three Counties. One Agent.

Volusia County

DeBary, Orange City, Deltona

I-4 access, affordable land relative to Orange County, and a growing residential base creating commercial demand. Early-mover advantage still available in select corridors.

Seminole County

Sanford, Lake Mary, Longwood

One of Central Florida's strongest commercial markets. Sanford's historic downtown is undergoing real revitalization. Lake Mary remains a corporate office anchor.

Lake County

Mt Dora, Eustis, Leesburg, Tavares

Rural character, lake-centric lifestyle, and significant land available for agricultural and commercial development. Mt Dora's tourist economy supports retail.
